Elephant Facts

Learn about these gentle giants.

  • Largest land mammals, weighing up to 14,000 lbs
  • Trunks have over 40,000 muscles for dexterity
  • Live in matriarchal herds led by females
  • Life expectancy: 60-70 years in the wild
